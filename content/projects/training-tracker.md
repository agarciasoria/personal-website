## Overview

The Track & Field Training Tracker is a custom-built web application designed specifically for the nuanced needs of track and field athletes. It goes beyond basic fitness tracking by allowing athletes to structure their training into macro-cycles, distinguish between track running and gym weightlifting, and visualize their progress over time.

## Key Features

- **Training Cycles:** Organize your season into structured training blocks (e.g., Pre-Season, Competition).
- **Dual Workout Modes:** 
  - 🏃 **Track Mode:** Log distances, run times, and recovery intervals, with visual cues for varying recovery lengths.
  - 🏋️ **Gym Mode:** Log sets, reps, and weights to track strength progression.
- **Advanced Analytics:** Dynamic, interactive charts powered by Chart.js. Visualize time vs. distance for track workouts or weight progression for gym routines.
- **Cloud Sync & Authentication:** Secure Google Sign-in with real-time data syncing across devices using Firebase Firestore.
- **Detailed Filtering:** Easily search and summarize past day entries by cycle, date, or workout type.

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend:** Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and responsive CSS
- **Backend & Database:** Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore (NoSQL)
- **Data Visualization:** Chart.js for interactive progress plotting
